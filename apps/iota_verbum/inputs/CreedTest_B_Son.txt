And in one Lord Jesus Christ the only begotten Son of God begotten of the Father before all worlds Light from Light true God from true God begotten not made of one substance with the Father by whom all things were made
Who for us and for our salvation came down from heaven and was incarnate by the Holy Spirit of the virgin Mary and was made man
He was crucified for us under Pontius Pilate suffered and was buried and on the third day He rose again according to the Scriptures
He ascended into heaven and sits at the right hand of the Father and He shall come again with glory to judge the living and the dead whose kingdom shall have no end
