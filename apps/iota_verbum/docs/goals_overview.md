# Iota Verbum – Goals 1–3 Overview

## Goal 1 – Find every hinge (◇ → Δ)

Build data + code to detect, store, and list □ / ◇ / Δ hinges across the canon (or another corpus).
This includes canonical hinge seeds, micro-hinges, and tools to query them by type, theme, and parent.

## Goal 2 – Understand what hinges reveal

Formalise how hinges disclose who God is, how He acts, and how creation should respond.
Provide schemas and services that group hinges and expose patterns (by covenant, by theme, by Christ-fulfilment).

## Goal 3 – Teach machines and people

Expose hinge-logic to machines (APIs, ethics gates, policy layers) and to people (docs, catechesis tools, CLI helpers),
so that right action flows from right being and remains accountable to the revealed character of God.
