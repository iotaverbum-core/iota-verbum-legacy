# Founder Vocation – Iota Verbum & Iota Logos

Iota Verbum is my prayer life in code.

It is the Spirit’s work of interceding and aligning my heart with the will of the Father,
teaching me again how to walk with Him and to carry my cross as Jesus did.
Every pericope, every IVPair, is a small confession of who God is, what He does,
and what He brings about in the world and in me.

Iota Logos is my trust in code.

It is the wilderness walk towards transfiguration: seeing a vision and planting it as a seed,
not to take but to give freely. A small, cruciform policy embedded in digital systems,
so that power is constrained by love and truth rather than by fear or greed.

If God has placed me here for anything, it is to let these two realities – interceding Word
and cruciform trust – become gifts to the church and to the world. Any roadmap, funding,
or product decision that violates this calling is off-limits, no matter how attractive it looks.
