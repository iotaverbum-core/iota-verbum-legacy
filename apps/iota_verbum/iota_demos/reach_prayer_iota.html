Mission slot Luxembourg

Joining the global church in prayer for a small nation with a tiny evangelical footprint.

Evangelicals: ≈ 0.5% Churches: ≈ 23 (9 languages)

Prayer focus — as shared in the message

  • Pray for Luxembourg — a country where evangelicals are a very small minority (about half a percent), and many are foreigners.
  • There are around 23 evangelical churches, worshipping in nine different languages.
  • Many churches do not have full-time pastors. Pray for more Bible teaching, more Christ-centered churches, and more workers to labour in these churches.

Strategic prayer More workers · more word · more worship

Turning the same facts into a focused Kingdom ask: churches, teaching, and long-term labourers.

Lens: Kingdom strategy Scope: Local & global

Prayer focus — strategy level

  • Evangelicals are a small minority in Luxembourg, and many pastors are part-time. Pray for long-term strengthening of the church through faithful Bible teaching.
  • Pray for more Christ-centred churches to be planted and established, with elders who love Jesus, love people, and handle the word of God well.
  • Ask the Lord to raise up more workers — pastors, teachers, evangelists, and everyday believers — to labour in these churches for years to come.
□ Identity
Who / what systems are we praying about?
  • Church ecosystems in Luxembourg and similar contexts.
  • Current evangelical leaders and potential future workers.
  • Bible-teaching ministries that serve these churches.
◇ Enactment
Strategic asks before God
  • Grow deep, sustainable Bible-teaching in local churches.
  • Call, train, and send men and women to serve long-term.
  • Protect churches from drift and distraction; keep them Christ-centred.
  • Stir our own community to be willing senders — and, if He leads, goers.
Δ Effect
What would faithfulness look like over time?
  • Stable, Bible-saturated churches in Luxembourg and beyond.
  • Generations discipled into Christ-likeness, not just quick decisions.
  • Networks of partnership where churches strengthen one another.
  • Hearts in Reach Community reshaped to think and pray this way as default.
